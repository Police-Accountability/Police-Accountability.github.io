CURRENT WORK
PoliceAccountability.com partners with local grassroots organizations and community leaders, enabling us to deploy national resources at the local level where they are most effective.
PoliceAccountability.com is currently working with local activists and BLM organizers to document and investigate reported incidents in the following cities:
Seattle, WA
Atlanta, GA
Louisville, KY
Breonna Taylor - Louisville, KY
Currently we are working in the Louisville, KY area in response to the killing of Breonna Taylor by members of the Louisville Metro Police Department (LMPD). We have partnered with local BLM organizers and the attorneys for the Breonna Taylor family in an effort to understand what actions led to her wrongful death. PoliceAccountability.com is currently conducting a city-wide survey to gain insight into community sentiment toward local law enforcement, including excessive use of force, potential corruption, systemic racism, and housing displacement due to gentrification.
‍
If you are a member of the Louisville community and would like to participate in the survey, please contact us directly. We want to hear from you.
If you or someone you know has been a victim of police excessive use of force, abuse of power, or corruption, we want to hear from you.
